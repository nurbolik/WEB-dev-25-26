Recipe #1 – Burger

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Bun 1
Meat patty 1
Cheese 1 slice
Lettuce 2 leaves

Step 1: Fry the meat patty.

Step 2: Cut the bun and warm it.

Step 3: Put meat, cheese, and lettuce inside.

Step 4: Close the bun and eat.
