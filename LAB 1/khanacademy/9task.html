WELCOME TO BALI

Bali is the most beautiful island in Indonesia! The weather is always warm and sunny. White sand beaches and palm trees are waiting for you in Bali!

Things to do in Bali:

  • Sunbathe on the beach
  • Swim in the ocean
  • Eat delicious tropical fruits
  • Watch amazing sunsets
  • Visit ancient temples
  • Try surfing

Best places to visit:

  1. Kuta Beach
  2. Tanah Lot Temple
  3. Ubud Rice Terraces
  4. Mount Batur Volcano
  5. Seminyak
  6. Lovina Beach

Travel tip: Don't forget sunscreen! The Balinese sun is very strong.

What to pack:

  • Light clothes (shorts, t-shirts)
  • Swimsuit
  • Sunglasses
  • Comfortable shoes
  • Camera
  • A big smile!

Bali is waiting for you! This will be your best vacation ever!
